Da
==
datsi - food
dahsō - drink (n) / water
daehi - alive (+ohē)
doh   - yes
daet  - good

Ta
==
tau   - no
tauhi - dead (+ohē)
taut  - bad

Say
===
sō   - this/that
say  - animal
sē   - he/she/they
sed  - you
sī   - me
saed - girl
saut - boy

Ōhē
===
ōhē   - do
doh   - go (+da)
tō'ē  - stop (+ta)
hie   - life
ōhtsi - eat (+da)
ōdsō  - drink (+da)
