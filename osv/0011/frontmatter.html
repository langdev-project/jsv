Frontmatter

CLASSICAL SEVENSI is the form of SEVENSI in use from the beginning of written records through the publishing of the various Classics. In addition to providing the basis for SEVENSI as a whole, many aspects of CLASSICAL SEVENSI are preserved in the "classical" speech-level.

This dictionary records the lexicon for CLASSICAL SEVENSI XI, in which the phonological systems were further standardized and the grammars matured.

Abbreviations used:

  • dim. : diminutive (of)
  • lit. : literally
  • VIV : CLASSICAL SEVENSI VIV
  • X : CLASSICAL SEVENSI X
  • XI : CLASSICAL SEVENSI XI
