Frontmatter

CLASSICAL SEVENSI is the form of SEVENSI in use from the beginning of written records through the publishing of the various Classics. In addition to providing the basis for SEVENSI as a whole, many aspects of CLASSICAL SEVENSI are preserved in the "classical" speech-level.

This dictionary records the lexicon for CLASSICAL SEVENSI X, the first level of formalization and standardization from PROTO–JASTU-SEVENSI.

Abbreviations used:

  • dim. : diminutive (of)
  • lit. : literally
  • VIV : CLASSICAL SEVENSI VIV
  • X : CLASSICAL SEVENSI X

CLASSICAL SEVENSI X has been given the language code art-x-osv-0010.

CLASSICAL SEVENSI X is currently under active development, and the entries contained herein are subject to change at any time.
